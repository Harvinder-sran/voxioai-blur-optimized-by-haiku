FAQ

Common questions. Clear answers.

Do the agents really sound human?
Yes — listen to the recordings above. We tune voice, pacing, and interruption handling until callers can't tell they're talking to AI.
How long does setup take?
A first agent is usually live within 1–2 weeks, depending on integrations and call complexity.
What languages are supported?
English, Hindi, and major Indian + global languages — including mixed-language calls.
Can it transfer to a human?
Yes. Agents warm-transfer to your team with full context whenever a call needs a person.
How much does it cost to run?
A flat monthly plan covers build and management; call minutes are included up to your tier. See pricing above.
